About Us

ABOUT US

Persatuan Belia Perkampungan Permatang Tinggi is a youth organisation focused on leadership development, community service, cultural activities and volunteerism since 1970.

We empower youths through meaningful programmes, volunteerism, and social initiatives in Permatang Tinggi, Penang.
